cummax

Cumulative maximum

Calling Sequence

M = cummax(A)
M = cummax(A,dim)
M = cummax(_,direction)

Parameters

A:

Input Array. Can be real or complex and a vector or matrix. For complex elements, cummax compares the magnitude of elements. If the magnitude are same, phase angles are compared.

dim:

Dimension to operate along. If no dimension is specified, then the default value is the first array dimension whose value is greater than 1. Should be a natural number.

direction:

Direction of operation. String flag - 'forward' (default) or 'reverse'

Examples

// Cumulative maximum values in a vector
v = [8 9 1 10 6 1 3 6 10 10]
M = cummax(v)

// Expected output: [8 8 1 1 1 1 1 1 1 1]
